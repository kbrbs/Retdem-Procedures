What is Retention Dam?

A retention dam, also known as a retention basin or detention dam, is a structure designed to temporarily store water for various purposes including flood control, water supply, and sediment management. This educational resource covers essential procedures, design principles, construction techniques, and safety considerations for retention dam systems.

Key Topics Covered

  • Site selection and geological assessment
  • Hydraulic design and calculations
  • Construction materials and methods
  • Safety and environmental considerations
  • Maintenance and monitoring procedures
