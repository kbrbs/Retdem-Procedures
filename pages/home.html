Why Return Demonstrations Matter

A Return Demonstration (RetDem) is a vital educational method in nursing where a student exhibits their understanding and mastery of a clinical skill by performing it in front of an instructor after being taught. This hands-on evaluation bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world clinical challenges.

Key Benefits

  • Instills confidence in executing procedures safely
  • Develops critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Encourages real-time feedback and correction
  • Prepares students to deliver high-quality patient care
