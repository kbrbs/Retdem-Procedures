Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with the RetDem program?

You can reach out to us through the contact form above or visit the resources page for more information about volunteer opportunities with our nursing students.

Is the program open to students from other institutions?

While primarily designed for our nursing students, we welcome inquiries from other institutions about collaboration opportunities.

How often are activities conducted?

Our RetDem activities are scheduled regularly throughout the academic year. Visit our interactive map to see upcoming activities in your area.

Can healthcare organizations partner with us?

Absolutely! We're always looking for meaningful partnerships. Please contact us to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

How can I access the resources?

Visit our Resources page to download educational materials, guides, and other helpful information about community health nursing.

Do you offer certificates or credentials?

Yes, students who complete the program receive certificates of completion and documentation of their community health service hours.
