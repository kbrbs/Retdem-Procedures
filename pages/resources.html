Quick Reference

Standard Vital Signs

  • Blood Pressure: Normal is less than 120/80 mmHg
  • Heart Rate: Normal is 60-100 beats per minute
  • Respiration: Normal is 12-20 breaths per minute
  • Temperature: Normal is 98.6°F (37°C)

Emergency Contacts

  • Emergency Services: 911 (USA)
  • Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222 (USA)
  • Mental Health Crisis: 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)

Infection Control Basics

  • Hand hygiene is the first line of defense
  • Use personal protective equipment (PPE) when needed
  • Follow standard precautions for all patients
  • Dispose of sharps and biohazardous waste properly
